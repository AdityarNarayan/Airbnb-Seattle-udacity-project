A Deep Dive Into Seattle Airbnb Market-Data Science Project

Aditya Narayan
May 3 · 5 min read

Introduction

First lets know what Airbnb does?

Airbnb, Inc. is an online marketplace for arranging or offering lodging, primarily homestays, or tourism experiences. The company does not own any of the real estate listings, nor does it host events; it acts as a broker, receiving commissions from each booking.

Being a Data Science enthusiast it was a exciting experience to dive into Airbnb Seattle business and pursue my first Data science project offered by Udacity. Source of data is from Airbnb website which provided information about listings of Airbnb in Seattle, Listing Calendar and Customer reviews.

In this article we are going to outline the findings to the bellow questions and we will also find the factors that are impacting price of Airbnb listings.

Airbnb pricing trends in Seattle:

  1. How does pricing fluctuates across different months of the year?
  2. How does pricing increase or decrease by neighborhood and which ones are the priciest neighborhoods in Seattle?
  3. How does price of different type of property and neighborhoods in seattle are related to each other?

Lets answer the above questions:

  1. How does pricing fluctuates across different months of the year?

I have constructed a line chart that depict the price trend of Airbnb in Seattle during different months across the year. We can see in the below charts that the prices are low in the beginning of the year that is technically the time of New Year celebration and there is a sudden hike in the price after June month. But how does this happened that the prices are low during the New Year?

Lets find out the answers:

  1. Why prices are low in the starting of the year?
    There could be several reasons that can impact the low prices in the beginning of the year. But if we have a look on the chart, we will see that there are quite good numbers of bookings at end of the year. With the help of this we can draw a conclusion that people plan there vacation during Christmas time and return to there homes before New Year. Second reason could be that people spent a lot of money during Christmas so, they don’t want to take extra financial burden in the first Q1 of the Year. So the primary reason for the low prices could be the less number of bookings in the starting of the year due to above two reasons.
  2. Why there is a hike in price after June month?
    I use google skills and find that the idle to visit Seattle is between June to October. The sudden hike in the prices are telling the same story to us. Hence , we can say that number of bookings is directly proportional to the price

2. How does pricing increase or decrease by neighborhood and which ones are the priciest neighborhoods in Seattle?

The above figure tell us a very simple thing that there is huge distribution of price around the Seattle neighborhood. The most expensive neighborhoods were Southeast Magnolia with an avg. price of $231 followed by Portage Bay at $227. The cheaper neighborhoods were Olympic Hills at $71 and Rainier Beach at $68. So next time if you plan your visit to Seattle you can consider the the cheaper place that cost you less or else if want to leave a luxurious life you can go for expensive places.

3. How does price of different type of property and neighborhoods in Seattle are related to each other?

To answer this question first we need to find what are the properties available in Seattle Airbnb listings and then we will try to establish a relation between 5 priciest neighborhood and two most rented properties in Seattle.

From the property type chart we can easily say that House and Apartment are the most rented properties in Seattle. Someone who is interested in listing a property in Seattle they can refer this finding and can invest wisely in theses property types.

From fig 2. we already know that Southeast Magnolia, Portage Bay, Westlake, West Queen Anne and Montlake are the 5 most priciest property in Seattle Airbnb listings.

Findings from the above fig is quite interesting. From the figure we can say that Portage Bay is having more expensive Houses however, much cheaper apartments followed by West Queen Anne which is following the same trend. It is also interesting to note that the prices of Houses and Apartment are same in Westlake.

Factors that are impacting Airbnb listings price

Finally I had an interest in knowing the factors that are impacting the price of Airbnb listings in Seattle. So after some data preparation and data wrangling I was able to implement my Linear Regression model to Airbnb data set.

From my Linear Regression model I came across the factors that impacting price of Airbnb listings. I found that Property Location, Propert Type and host_neighbourhood are having more impact on deciding the price of Airbnb listings in Seattle however host_since, host_about, require_license and transit are having least impact on price of listings.

Conclusion

On the basis of my analysis, I would say Seattle is a popular destination to visit after June month. During this summer, prices are on peak however prices may vary among different neighborhood. So if you are planning to travel Seattle you can consider this blog for which neighborhood you want to visit and in which property type you want to stay. List of impacting factors above also helps you to understand the price of your accommodation.
