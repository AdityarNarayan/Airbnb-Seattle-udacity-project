A Deep Dive Into Seattle Airbnb Market-Data Science Project

Aditya Narayan
May 3 · 4 min read

Introduction

First lets know what Airbnb does?

Airbnb, Inc. is an online marketplace for arranging or offering lodging, primarily homestays, or tourism experiences. The company does not own any of the real estate listings, nor does it host events; it acts as a broker, receiving commissions from each booking.

Being a Data Science enthusiast it was a exciting experience to dive into Airbnb Seattle business and pursue my first Data science project offered by Udacity. Source of data is from Airbnb website which provided information about listings of Airbnb in Seattle, Listing Calendar and Customer reviews.

In this article we are going to outline the findings to the bellow questions:

Airbnb pricing trends in Seattle:

  1. How does pricing increase or decrease by neighborhood and which ones are the priciest neighborhoods in Seattle?
  2. How does pricing fluctuates across different months of the year?
  3. 3. How does type of property imapct the price in different neighborhood?

Airbnb listing price prediction for Seattle:

  1. Can we predict the price for a given listing?
  2. What factors of the listing correlate best for predicting price?

Lets See the pricing trends in Seattle:

We will see how the price will vary demographically and different reasons that will impact the price of accommodations.

The above figure tell us a very simple thing that there is huge distribution of price around the Seattle neighborhood. The most expensive neighborhoods were Southeast Magnolia with an avg. price of $231 followed by Portage Bay at $227. The cheaper neighborhoods were Olympic Hills at $71 and Rainier Beach at $68. So next time if you plan your visit to Seattle you can consider the the cheaper place that cost you less or else if want to leave a luxurious life you can go expensive places.

Findings from the above fig is quite interesting. From the figure we can say that Portage Bay is more expensive followed by West Queen Anne. It is also interesting to note that the prices of Houses and Apartment are same in Westlake.

Price Prediction of Seattle Airbnb

Finally I had an interest in predicting the price of Airbnb listings in Seattle. So after some data preparation and data wrangling I was able to implement my Linear Regression model to Airbnb data set.

From my Linear Regression model I came across the factors that impacting price of Airbnb listings. I found that host details such as the name of the host, about the host, picture of the host etc also have an impact on price of listings. Availability is also one of a key factor in deciding the prices.

Conclusion

On the basis of my analysis, I would say Seattle is a popular destination to visit after June month. During this summer, prices are on peak however prices may vary among different neighborhood. So if you are planning to travel Seattle you can consider this blog for which neighborhood you want to visit and in which property type you want to stay. List of impacting factors above also helps you to understand the price of your accommodation.
